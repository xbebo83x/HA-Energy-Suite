\# HA Energy Suite - Brand Guidelines



Version: 1.0



\---



\# Project Name



Official name:



\*\*HA Energy Suite\*\*



Short name:



\*\*HAES\*\*



\---



\# Mission



Build the most complete open-source energy suite for Home Assistant.



\---



\# Slogan



\*\*Understand your energy. Maximize your savings.\*\*



\---



\# Vision



HA Energy Suite helps Home Assistant users understand energy production, consumption and real economic savings through modern dashboards, advanced analytics and simple installation.



\---



\# Brand Values



\- Simplicity

\- Reliability

\- Transparency

\- Open Source

\- Professional Design

\- Community Driven



Every component of the project should reflect these values.



\---



\# Visual Identity



Style:



\- Modern

\- Minimal

\- Technical

\- Clean

\- Dark-first



Avoid overly decorative graphics.



The interface should remain clean, readable and professional.

\# Typography



Preferred font style:



\- Bold titles

\- Clean sans-serif fonts

\- High readability



Avoid decorative fonts.



\---



\# Documentation Style



Documentation must be:



\- concise

\- beginner friendly

\- technically accurate

\- easy to navigate



\---



\# Naming Convention



Use English for:



\- folders

\- filenames

\- documentation

\- variables

\- comments



Examples:



\- monthly-report.yaml

\- yearly-history.yaml

\- solar-overview.yaml



Avoid abbreviations when possible.



\---



\# Philosophy



HA Energy Suite is not just a dashboard.



It is an open-source ecosystem dedicated to helping Home Assistant users understand their energy, optimize consumption and maximize real savings.



Every new feature should answer one question:



> Does this help users better understand or manage their energy?



If the answer is no, it probably doesn't belong in this project.



\---



\# Long-Term Goal



Become the reference open-source energy suite for Home Assistant users worldwide.
